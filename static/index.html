About SASE @ U of M

The Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers is a nation-wide organization with the mission of providing opportunities for scientists and engineers of Asian heritage to enhance their professional skills and to connect them with industry-leading companies. Our chapter aims to create a community of students at Michigan, and act as a gateway to workshops, volunteering events, company information sessions and much more. We welcome students of all backgrounds to not only take advantage of the numerous events we organize, but also to help us in making these events possible!
