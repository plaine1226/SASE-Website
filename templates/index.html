Welcome to SASE!

Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers at the University of Michigan

Why You Should Join

Connect

We believe that true connections are the foundation of personal and professional growth. We provide a vibrant community where like-minded individuals come together to forge meaningful connections that last a lifetime. Whether you're looking to expand your network, enhance your skills, or simply meet inspiring people, we have created the perfect environment for you.

Grow

Through our carefully curated events, workshops, and seminars, we offer a diverse range of opportunities to expand your knowledge, skills, and perspectives. Attend our expert-led seminars, where industry leaders share their insights and experiences. Engage in interactive workshops designed to enhance your personal effectiveness, leadership abilities, and communication skills.

Innovate

SASE is a hub for knowledge-sharing and continuous learning. Our club hosts workshops, guest speaker events, and skill-building sessions that enhance your creative thinking abilities. Learn practical techniques for idea generation, problem-solving, and effective communication. Expand your toolkit of creative methods and gain the confidence to think outside the box.

Our Mission

The Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers is a nation-wide organization with the mission of providing opportunities for scientists and engineers of Asian heritage to enhance their professional skills and to connect them with industry-leading companies. Our chapter aims to create a community of students at Michigan, and act as a gateway to workshops, volunteering events, company information sessions and much more. We welcome students of all backgrounds to not only take advantage of the numerous events we organize, but also to help us in making these events possible!
